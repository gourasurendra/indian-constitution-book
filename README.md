# 🇮🇳 Indian Constitution Book

A modern, interactive web platform designed to explore and understand the **Constitution of India** in a simple, structured, and engaging way. This project transforms constitutional content into a clean digital experience that’s easy to navigate for everyone—from students to competitive exam aspirants.

## 🏛️ About the Constitution

The Constitution of India is the **supreme law of the land**, adopted on **26 November 1949** and enforced on **26 January 1950**. It defines:

* Fundamental Rights
* Duties of citizens
* Structure of government
* Powers of institutions

It is the **longest written constitution in the world**.
